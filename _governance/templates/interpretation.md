## CONSENT REQUIREMENTS

Valid consent must include: system identity, specific task, domain, duration, and memory permission. Consent automatically expires at task completion or duration end, whichever occurs first.

## CONSENT CONSTRAINTS

## BOUNDARY ENFORCEMENT
