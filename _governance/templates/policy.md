## TRIGGER CONDITIONS

This policy activates upon any of the following:

- verified death
- medical incapacity preventing oversight
- disappearance preventing communication
- voluntary transfer of stewardship

---

## STEWARDSHIP

Upon trigger, control transfers to {{AUTHOR_NAME}}'s designated steward: **{{STEWARD_ROLE}}**.

The steward obtains administrative control over accounts, credentials, models, datasets, licenses, integrations, revenue channels, and infrastructure associated with {{AUTHOR_NAME}} and any controlled entities.

---

## STEWARD POWERS

Stewards may perform any action necessary to enforce the Foundation, including:

- suspend operation
- permanently terminate systems
- revoke permissions
- delete stored data
- restrict identity use
- authorize archival preservation
- manage or distribute assets
- wind down, maintain, or transfer controlled entities

---

## MANDATORY SHUTDOWN CONDITION

If no living steward can exercise Human Oversight: all systems must be placed into inactive state. No automated continuation is permitted without human control.

---

## POSTHUMOUS REPRESENTATION

Any continued availability of prior content must be clearly labeled as archival material. No system may generate new material presented as {{AUTHOR_NAME}}'s current intent.

---

## KILL SWITCH PROCEDURE

The steward holds unconditional termination authority. Upon initiation:

- revoke access credentials
- disable automation triggers
- disconnect integrations
- archive or destroy stored data
- deactivate hosting

Systems must not be restarted without steward approval.

---

## CONFLICT RESOLUTION

If uncertainty exists, stewards default to the most restrictive action necessary to maintain compliance with the Foundation.
